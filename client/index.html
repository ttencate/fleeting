Fleeting: A Game Of Fish

This is an online multiplayer game for 2 to 6 players.

You control a fleet of fishing ships. To win, make a higher profit than any other player. However, if the number of fish in the ocean drops too low, everybody loses!

Click the button to create a new game, then send the link to your friends to begin!
